Odoo-first implementation plan — 34 installed apps

This is the corrected strategy: Odoo native feature → enterprise/global workflow → MLG mapping → MLH mapping → safe action or approval gate. Research sources: Odoo 19 official docs and Odoo construction industry workflow page, plus live Odoo audit.

Corrected
Operating rule: no more MLG/MLH-only random setup. Each app below is handled from the Odoo feature model first. Money/legal/payroll/permissions/customer-facing sends stay approval-gated.

Contacts

ACTIVE

Odoo function: Customers, vendors, subcontractors, landowners, companies, addresses, tags, portal/contact identity.

Enterprise pattern: Enterprise master data governance: dedupe, classify, protect, and connect contacts to CRM/sales/accounting/projects.

MLG: MLG customers, investors, vendors, subcontractors, partners.

MLH: MLH homeowners, legacy clients, warranty contacts, vendors.

Next: ACTIVE: categories created; next dedupe/import verified contacts only.

Accounting

HOLD

Odoo function: Journals, taxes, chart of accounts, customer/vendor accounting, financial reports.

Enterprise pattern: Enterprise close discipline: clean fiscal localization, bank reconciliation, analytic dimensions, approval segregation.

MLG: MLG clean company books and job-cost reporting.

MLH: MLH legacy accounting/docs separated; old issues reviewed before import.

Next: HOLD: accountant/tax/bank approval required before final setup.

Invoicing

HOLD

Odoo function: Customer invoices, vendor bills, payment status, credit notes.

Enterprise pattern: Draft-to-approval-to-post controls; no casual invoice posting.

MLG: Draft milestone/deposit invoices only after sales/contract approval.

MLH: Legacy invoices imported only as evidence until reviewed.

Next: HOLD: no posted invoices/payments without approval.

CRM

ACTIVE
